Run a Profitable Fundraiser with a Local Company and Local Products

At Stevco, we make it possible for your to raise funds through the sale of quality products, most of which are made locally by small businesses.

Our philosophy is simple. If you sell something people want to buy, they will buy more of it. This means it will be easier for your group to raise money.

Additionally, our programs all repeat very well, with many groups seeing a growth in profit from year to year. This is because of the quality of the items we choose.

Profit Scale

We keep your profit simple. You'll earn between $5 and $7 per item, depending on how many items your group sells overall.

Small groups can expect to make between $1,000 - $3,000 per sale, while larger groups like schools and sports leagues can easily make between $6,000 - $10,000.

Smaller groups especially appreciate our no minimum free delivery policy.
